Collaboration Agreement Between Net Nirvana Studios and Algorithm Cat

Date:

Parties Involved:

  • Net Nirvana Studios (hereinafter referred to as "NNS")
  • Algorithm Cat (hereinafter referred to as "AC")

Purpose:

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions under which AC will bring web app development projects to NNS. NNS will be responsible for the development and delivery of these projects according to the terms specified below.

1. Payment Terms

1.1 Project Charges:

NNS will charge between 30% to 40% of the total project amount.

1.2 Advance Payment:

NNS will require 50% of the total project cost as an advance payment before the commencement of any project.

1.3 Final Payment:

The remaining 50% will be paid upon project completion and client approval.

2. Project Contracts

2.1 Contract Requirement:

Each project will have a formal contract signed by both NNS and the client brought in by AC. The contract will detail the scope, deliverables, timelines, payment schedules, and any other relevant terms.

2.2 Legal Binding:

All contracts will be legally binding and must be adhered to by both parties.

3. Project Timelines

3.1 Time Estimates:

Each project will have a predefined timeline, agreed upon in the contract. NNS will provide realistic time estimates based on the project scope and complexity.

3.2 Milestones:

Projects will be divided into milestones, with regular updates provided to AC and the client.

4. Technology Stack

4.1 Preferred Technologies:

NNS will work exclusively with the following technologies:

  • MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js)
  • Next.js
  • Java Spring

4.2 Flexibility:

If a project requires technologies outside the preferred stack, it will be discussed on a case-by-case basis and may incur additional costs.

5. Communication and Updates

5.1 Regular Updates:

NNS will provide regular progress updates to AC and the client, ensuring transparency throughout the project lifecycle.

5.2 Communication Channels:

Preferred communication channels will include email, project management tools, and scheduled video conferences.

6. Quality Assurance

6.1 Testing:

All projects will undergo thorough testing before delivery to ensure they meet the agreed-upon standards and specifications.

6.2 Revisions:

Any necessary revisions or bug fixes identified during the testing phase will be addressed promptly by NNS.

7. Confidentiality

7.1 Confidential Information:

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary or sensitive information exchanged during the collaboration.

7.2 Non-Disclosure:

A Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) will be signed by both parties to protect confidential information.

8. Termination

8.1 Termination Clause:

Either party may terminate this agreement with [insert notice period] written notice if the other party fails to comply with the terms outlined in this document.

8.2 Outstanding Payments:

In the event of termination, any outstanding payments for work completed by NNS will be due immediately.

9. Additional Points

9.1 Dispute Resolution:

Any disputes arising from this collaboration will be resolved through mediation and, if necessary, arbitration.

9.2 Intellectual Property:

All intellectual property developed during the project will belong to the client, with NNS retaining the right to showcase the work in their portfolio.

9.3 Project Management:

A dedicated project manager from NNS will oversee each project to ensure timely delivery and adherence to specifications.
